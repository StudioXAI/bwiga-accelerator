01 · The infrastructure

What are Market Rails?

The infrastructure layer behind market activation.

Market rails are the rules, systems and protocol logic that help a project move from fundraising to live market activity. In the BWiGA ecosystem, this means a connected path from launch to listing, from listing to protected trading, and from early trading to liquidity activation.

Instead of treating launch, listing and liquidity as separate problems, INFI MultiChain connects them into one structured infrastructure flow.

Launch

Structured fundraising and launch execution through predefined protocol logic.

Listing

A path toward CDEX listing after launch and readiness conditions are met.

Trading

Non-custodial trading supported by market protection logic.

Liquidity

Liquidity floor, lock logic, future settlement layers and cross-chain liquidity direction.

Launch is not the finish line. Market survival is.
02 · Launchpad

Launchpad

Automated launch execution for serious projects.

The INFI MultiChain Launchpad is designed to help projects launch with more structure, transparency and automation. Founders define the fundraising logic in advance — including price, timing, soft cap, hard cap and raise model — and the protocol executes the process.

The goal is to reduce manual dependency, remove hidden control and connect fundraising with market activation.

03 · Protected trading

CDEX & SbSe Shield

Protected trading infrastructure for early market survival.

The first phase after listing is often where strong projects lose momentum. Aggressive selling, weak liquidity, manipulation and panic can destroy trust before the project has time to grow.

INFI MultiChain CDEX is designed as a protected, non-custodial trading environment where listing, trading and protection logic operate inside one system. SbSe Shield adds protocol-level rules designed to reduce harmful manipulation, high liquidity impact and destructive early trading behavior.

Liquidity Floor

The Soft Cap can define the minimum liquidity percentage that becomes part of the CDEX listing liquidity.

Minimum 3-Month Liquidity Lock

Listing liquidity is locked at protocol level for a minimum of 3 months to support stronger early-market trust.

SbSe Shield

High-impact selling can trigger progressive protection fees designed to reduce destructive market pressure.

Compensation Logic

Protection fees can support compensation mechanisms for affected users, helping keep value inside the market ecosystem.

DAO-Controlled Unlock

Future liquidity unlocks can be governed by DAO approval instead of hidden manual control.

Trust is not promised after the damage. It is enforced by protocol rules before the market breaks.
04 · Future liquidity

InvertX & Liquidity Layer

Toward unified liquidity and settlement across chains.

InvertX is being developed as a Stable Digital Protocol Unit Asset inside the BWiGA ecosystem. Its purpose is to support future cross-chain settlement, liquidity coordination and market activity — without relying on classic stablecoin mechanics, centralized issuer logic or fragmented liquidity across chains.

Together with the future Liquidity Lending layer, the goal is to help projects access starting liquidity, reduce liquidity fragmentation and grow into real market demand through user activity.

01 · Stable Unit

Stable Digital Protocol Unit

InvertX is designed as a protocol-native unit for settlement and liquidity coordination, not as a traditional stablecoin.

02 · Direction

Cross-Chain Liquidity Direction

The goal is to reduce fragmented liquidity across chains and support one connected market layer.

03 · Lending
