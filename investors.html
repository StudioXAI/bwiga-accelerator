04 · The protection

Protected Market Thesis

Early markets should not be destroyed by their first liquidity shock.

Many token projects fail not because the idea is weak, but because the market collapses during the first phase after listing. BWiGA's protected market thesis is built around protocol-level rules designed to reduce harmful early-market manipulation, liquidity impact and destructive post-launch behavior.

Soft Cap Liquidity Floor

The Soft Cap can define the minimum liquidity percentage that becomes part of the listing liquidity.

3-Month Liquidity Lock

Listing liquidity is locked at protocol level for a minimum of 3 months, supporting stronger early-market trust.

SbSe Shield

High-impact selling can trigger progressive protection fees designed to reduce destructive market pressure.

Compensation Logic

Protection fees can support compensation mechanisms for affected users, helping keep value inside the market ecosystem.

DAO-Controlled Unlock

Later liquidity unlocks can be governed by DAO approval instead of hidden manual control.

BWiGA does not remove market risk. It reduces specific structural weaknesses that usually destroy early markets.
