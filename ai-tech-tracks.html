01 · The thesis

Why AI Tech Tracks

The real opportunity is not AI as a feature. It is AI connected to markets.

AI is moving from content and chat toward agents, automation, financial decision support, infrastructure, risk analysis and autonomous workflows.

At the same time, Web3 provides the rails for ownership, settlement, liquidity, tokenization, incentives and transparent market activity.

BWiGA Accelerator is built for founders who understand this convergence and are building systems that can connect intelligence with market infrastructure.

Agents Need Payments

Autonomous systems need wallets, permissions, settlement, micropayments and transaction logic.

AI Needs Infrastructure

Compute, data, storage, inference and decentralized resource networks are becoming core AI layers.

Finance Needs Automation

AI can support underwriting, risk, treasury, trading intelligence and market monitoring.

Markets Need Trust

AI-native systems require identity, verification, fraud detection, security and compliance logic.

Tokenization Needs Intelligence

RWA and synthetic asset markets can benefit from AI-driven analysis, monitoring and automation.

The future is not AI alone. It is AI connected to programmable markets.

03 · Deep dive

AI Agents & Payments

When AI agents act, they will need to transact.

AI agents are evolving from assistants into autonomous systems that can search, negotiate, subscribe, purchase, invoice, pay for APIs, access services and interact with digital markets. To act in the real economy, they need payment rails, wallet logic, permissions, settlement and trust infrastructure.

PROJECT TYPE 01

Agent Wallets

Wallet infrastructure designed for AI agents, permissions, user control and transaction automation.

PROJECT TYPE 02

AI Commerce

Tools enabling AI agents to buy, sell, subscribe or transact on behalf of users or businesses.

PROJECT TYPE 03

Machine-to-Machine Payments

Payment systems for agents, APIs, bots, services, devices and automated workflows.

PROJECT TYPE 04

Stablecoin Automation

Settlement tools using stable-value rails for instant, programmable and cross-border payments.

PROJECT TYPE 05

AI SaaS Billing

Usage-based billing and payment automation for AI tools, APIs, data services and software platforms.

Market Connection. AI payment projects may naturally connect to Web3 settlement, wallet infrastructure, programmable finance, stable-value systems and autonomous transaction networks — including, where relevant, BWiGA's Technology Partner stack (INFI MultiChain).

The agent economy will need more than intelligence. It will need rails for value movement.
