03 · The protection

Protected Market Entry

The first market phase should not depend on luck.

Many projects suffer most during the first phase after listing. A few aggressive sellers can create panic, damage the chart, drain confidence and push early supporters out of the market.

Through CDEX and SbSe Shield logic, selected projects may enter a market environment designed to reduce harmful manipulation, high-impact sells, liquidity shocks and destructive early trading behavior.

Liquidity Floor

The Soft Cap can define the minimum liquidity percentage that is added to the CDEX listing and locked at market activation.

Minimum Liquidity Lock

Listing liquidity is locked for a minimum of 3 months at protocol level, supporting stronger early-market trust.

SbSe Shield

High-impact sells can trigger progressive protection fees designed to reduce destructive market behavior.

Compensation Logic

Protection fees can support a compensation mechanism for affected users, helping keep value inside the market ecosystem.

DAO-Controlled Unlock

Future liquidity unlocks can be governed through DAO approval instead of hidden manual control.

BWiGA does not remove market risk. It reduces specific structural weaknesses that usually destroy early markets.
Protection is not about controlling the market. It is about creating a fair environment where real projects can grow.
